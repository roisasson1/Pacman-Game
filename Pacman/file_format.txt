Steps file:
The first integer is the level, 
The letters (a, A, d, D, w, W, x, X, s, S) are the moves of the game objects.
The sign '?' symboling the appearance of the fruit, and then the exact place of the fruit on the board,
and after that, the value that appears on the screen.
The sign '!' symboling the disapperance of the fruit.

Result file:
The last line is the remaining lives of this screen.
